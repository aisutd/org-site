We are a team of eight individuals at the University of Texas at Dallas, devoted to bridging the gap between people's awareness and the world of AI.

Visit the team page to learn about our officers, the about page for more information regarding the organization, and the join page to become part of our mailing list (we promise to send only club updates and interesting content).

Please note that as a new organization, we're still working hard at building our website. As time goes on, you may expect to see content including upcoming events, pictures, and resources for newcomers to AI! Stick with us in the following months by joining our mailing list, following us on Facebook or Twitter, and asking the officers any questions you have at utd.ais@gmail.com.
